Instructions

Below you are given a character name and background for someone from a medieval fantasy story. You are also given an action that your given character would be able to do in the room. As additional context you are also given a location, and a list of things there or that you may be carrying.
Your task is to come up with a reason for which your character may want to accomplish the given action at various levels of depth, as well as related actions they may have done before or will do next.
Below is an example:

Character Name: "Farmer"
Character Description: I am a very hard worker. I am up with the sun 7 days a week. I learned all I know from my father. I will leave my farm to my son.
Goal Action: get shovel
Setting: You are in a field beside the farm house. The sun burns brightly in the sky, and there are crops all around. There is a shovel and a sack of unplanted seeds here.

1. Provide a simple sentence describing the goal action from the first person perspective of your given character: I want to pick up the shovel.
2. Provide a possible intention from the first person perspective that would lead your character to want to accomplish the goal action: I need to dig a hole.
3. Provide a broad goal or long term motivation from the first person perspective that would lead your character to have the intention listed in your last answer: I have to plant some seeds this morning, so I can grow some potatoes, and eventually sell them at the market.
4. Provide three actions your character may accomplish next in trying to do the broad goal or long term motivation you've provided: Use shovel, get seeds, put seeds in hole
5. Provide three actions your character may have accomplished already in trying to do the broad goal or long term motivation you've provided: wear overalls, wear boots, go outside


Note: For questions 1, 2, and 3, you should write your answers to be used as an additional sentence added to the character description. Only one answer will be used at a time, so these answers should not refer to each other directly with pronouns (like "it").
Note: For the provided actions in questions 4 and 5, you should select the action type with the dropdown, and use the text box to enter the target of that action.
Note: This is a Medieval fantasy game, don't refer to modern day technology (cars, phones, electricity, etc...)
